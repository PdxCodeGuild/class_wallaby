Take a bite into culinary diversity. Travel to countryside in nearby Lakeland, and discover a fusion of fine dining and farm-to-table restaurants of historical southern native "cracker" cuisine. Then take a tour to Clearwater to enjoy fresh seafood as it comes straight from the pier to the table.
